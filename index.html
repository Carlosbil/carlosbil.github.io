Acerca de mí

Hi! Welcome to my website. I love learning new things and continuously strive to improve myself, dedicating my full effort to every project I am involved in. Not only that, but I have also learned about the different algorithms used in machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing, such as SVM, KNN, K-means, and K-medoids. \n In this field, it is crucial to continue learning, and I am always searching for the latest news in this environment and how to improve myself in order to deliver the best results. \n Currently, I am working at CDS, an HPE company, as a DevOps engineer while I am studying for my master's degree. As part of my job, I usually work with Python and Java as backend languages, and Angular for frontend development. I also have experience with Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, and many other tools that are used for Continuous Delivery/Continuous Integration (CD/CI).
